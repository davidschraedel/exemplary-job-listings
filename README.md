# exemplary-job-listings


These are examples, or exemplary approaches in thinking, of posted jobs/roles when there is an opening. These companies have thought for themselves, rather than copying a corporate template. Their approach is engaging and compelling. Each has their unique strengths. Together they show how teams can effectively vie for attention by simply considering what is most strategic in the hiring process and what a candidate would want to receive as context about the open role.


**excerpt**:
- this directory houses snippets that show specific exemplary ideas from job postings.

**full-text**:
- this directory houses full examples of interesting and likely effective job postings.
