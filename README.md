# exemplary-job-listings

With the increased complexity of technology in the hiring process, attending to the human experience of the candidate becomes equally as important as improving the technical effectiveness of the hiring pipeline. Considering the user experience of the job candidate is paramount in getting great talent that matches your organization. As well, job listings are an important part of an organization's brand communication and experience.

Within this repository are examples of, or exemplary approaches to, posted jobs/roles at a company. These companies have thought for themselves in communicating about their specified role and organization, rather than merely copying a corporate template. Their approach is engaging and compelling. Each has their unique strengths. Together they show how teams can effectively vie for attention by simply considering what is most strategic in the hiring process and what a candidate would want to receive as context about the open role.

**synthesized_general_principles.md** describes generalized ideas for communicating appropriately in a job description, synthesized at least partially from the example job listings in this repo.

**excerpt**:
- this directory houses snippets that show specific exemplary ideas from job postings.

**full-text**:
- this directory houses full examples of interesting and likely effective job postings.
